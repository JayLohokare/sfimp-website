Our Vision

Three pillars driving our mission to build a lasting musical community

🎵 Music Excellence

Technical mastery meets soul and emotion. Cross-genre compositions for Indian and Western audiences. Studio jams, complex compositions, and refined multi-take recordings.

Goal: 4 Music Pods | 50+ Musicians | 150+ Videos

👥 Community First

Decentralized community built to outlast any single person. Public jams at Salesforce Park, East Cut, SF City Hall. Small-scale acoustic sessions and experimental formats.

Goal: 3 Quarterly Events | 100+ Participation | 1K+ Annual Audience

🌟 Sustainable Growth

Non-profit model where revenue reinvests into production, venues, and organizing committee. Venue partnerships, sponsorships, workshops, and flagship events.

Model: Revenue → Production + Venues + Community
